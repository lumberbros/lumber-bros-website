## Frequently asked questions

### **How do I get a quote?**

Fill out our short form online or call us at (252) 495-0720. We'll get back to you within 24 hours. In many cases we can quote from photos — no visit required.

### **Will the price change after you start the job?**

No. We provide fixed, itemized quotes before we touch anything. What we quote is what you pay.

### **Do you work in tight spaces?**

That's our specialty. Small backyards, trees against structures, old growth in historic neighborhoods, limited gate access — these are the jobs we're built for.

### **Will you damage my lawn or landscaping?**

We climb and rig most jobs specifically to avoid this. No heavy equipment means no ruts, no crushed beds, no tracked-up yard.

### **Are you insured?**

Yes. We carry $2 million in general liability coverage on every job. You can request our certificate of insurance before we start.

### **Do you offer emergency service?**

Yes. If a storm has created an immediate hazard, call us directly at (252) 495-0720.

### Do you remove stumps too?

Stump grinding is available as an add-on. Just mention it when you request your quote and we'll include it.

### **What happens to the wood and debris?**

We haul everything away as part of the job. If you'd like to keep logs for firewood we'll cut them down to size. The rest gets milled into lumber, slabs, or other products through our woodworks operation — nothing goes to waste.

### Do I need a permit to remove a tree in Beaufort, Pitt, or Washington County?

It depends on your municipality and the species of tree. Some towns have local ordinances governing removal of certain protected species. We'll help you understand what applies to your property when we come out.
