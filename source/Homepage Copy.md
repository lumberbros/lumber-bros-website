## Fundamentally different

The chipper and landfill are defaults in the forestry industry. They’re efficient, profitable, and require zero imagination. The faster a crew reduces a tree to chips and moves on, the better the profit margin. It's not cynical. It’s just math. 

We'd rather build something with timber than see mulched or burned. So we designed Lumber Bros differently. The result is pricing that's fair. Not because we cut corners. Because we built a business that doesn't need to overcharge.

- We buy and maintain used equipment instead of financing new toys.
- We climb and rig to minimize heavy machinery and fuel.
- We mill the timber we remove, and pass the savings to our customers.
- We run lean, using technology to cut the overhead that bloats most small businesses.
